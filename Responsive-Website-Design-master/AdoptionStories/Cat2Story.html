Hailey & Fish

My husband and I brought Fish (formerly known as Wish) home on Christmas eve. Fish is a spoiled little guy who loves tuna-flavored wet food, head scratches and laying under the Christmas tree (He's the reason we still have the tree up in January). He is an avid bird video watcher and loves listening to music with his mom and dad (He prefers Otis Redding).

"Adopting Fish was the best decision we ever made. She brought so much joy and love into our lives."
- Fish Family
