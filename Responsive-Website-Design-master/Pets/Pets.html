Available Pets

Adoption Process

Before deciding to adopt a dog or cat, ask yourself if you are ready to take lifelong responsibility for them, both financially, in terms of living space, and emotionally. Adoption requires significant commitment from yourself, your family, and relevant parties. Please carefully consider before contacting HPA about adoption.

Are you ready? Follow these steps:

  1. Learn about the pet you want to adopt on the HPA website.
  2. Contact the volunteer in charge of the pet to learn more about them.
  3. Participate in the adoption interview.
  4. Prepare the necessary facilities, complete adoption paperwork, and pay the adoption fee to welcome the pet home.
  5. Regularly update on the pet's situation, especially in case of emergencies for timely advice.

Adoption Requirements

To adopt a pet, you need to meet some basic requirements:

  • Have enough financial resources to care for the pet in the long term.
  • Have a suitable, spacious, and safe living environment for the pet.
  • Be willing to spend time caring for and playing with the pet every day.
  • Commit to not abandoning or returning the pet when issues arise.
  • Obtain consent from all family members regarding the adoption.
